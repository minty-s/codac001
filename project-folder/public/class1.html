Class 1 – Measurement, Data & Geometry

📏 Measurement

Measurement helps us find out how long, tall, heavy, or hot something is. We use tools like a ruler to measure length and a clock to measure time!

What's longer? Book or pencil? How much longer is the book than the pencil?

📊 Data

Data is information we collect. Like how many red apples and green apples are in a basket. We can count, sort, and even draw it in a chart!

There are 5 green apples and 4 red apples

🔺 Geometry

Geometry is about shapes! Circles, squares, and triangles are all around us. We learn their names and where we can see them.
