🌱 Parts of a Plant

🌿 Roots

  • Hold the plant firmly in the soil.
  • Absorb water and minerals from the ground.
  • Help store food in some plants like carrots and radishes.

🌳 Stem

  • Supports the plant and keeps it upright.
  • Carries water and nutrients from the roots to other parts.
  • Transports food made by the leaves to other parts of the plant.

🍃 Leaves

  • Known as the "kitchen" of the plant.
  • Make food through a process called photosynthesis using sunlight, water, and air.
  • Release oxygen into the air which we breathe.

🌸 Flowers

  • The most colorful and attractive part of the plant.
  • Help the plant reproduce by making seeds.
  • Attract bees, butterflies, and other pollinators.
